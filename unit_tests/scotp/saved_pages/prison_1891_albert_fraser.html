Prison registers

Search registers of people admitted to Scottish prisons to 1921. Currently, registers for Perth Prison are available.

Read how to search prison registers and understand their contents in our prison registers guide, or how to sort and filter search results and how to activate search tips on this page.

 

Often a prisoner will have more than one surname listed in the register, e.g. Agnes Smith or Brown, or George Anderson or Jones. For females, the second surname will often (but not always) be a married surname.

A surname search for Smith, for example, will return results for a prisoner named Agnes Smith or Brown, and also for a prisoner named Agnes Brown or Smith.

No punctuation is included in the surname results, so the surname O'NEILL, for example, will be recorded as ONEILL.

All ‘Mc’ surnames have been expanded to MAC to account for wide inconsistencies in the rendering of Mc, Mac and M' names by prison staff.

Search Options

Remember that forename spelling can vary.

Improve your search by selecting a forename search option, such as 'Names that begin with'.

For help in choosing the right forename option, read our guidance on forenames

Search Options
Gender:

Gender is set to 'Both' as the default option.

Select male or female to narrow your search.

Enter an age range to narrow your search. You can narrow or widen the age range on the results page.

If you enter an age in the first box only, all ages from the specified age will be included. If you enter an age in the second box only, all ages to the specified age will be included. If you leave both blank, all ages will be included.

Enter a range of years to narrow your search. You can narrow or widen the year range on the results page.

If you enter a year in the first box only, all years from the specified year will be included. If you enter a year in the second box only, all years to the specified year will be included. If you leave both blank, all years will be included.

Select a prison to narrow your search.

At present, registers for Perth Prison are available to search. For males, years available to search are 1867-1879, 1888-1897 and 1902-1921. For females, years available are 1901-1916. Other Perth Prison registers for the period up to 1921 have not survived.

Select a country of birth to narrow your search.

The results page provides the prisoner's place of birth, as recorded in the prison register. You can sort the 'Where born' column alphabetically on the results page.

Some early registers do not record a country or place of birth. Occasionally a prisoner did not provide a place of birth, or prison staff missed off this information. Selecting 'Outside UK/Ireland or Not Recorded' will locate these entries. Where no information was recorded, the entry will show -----. Where the place of birth was stated as unknown, the entry will show 'Unknown' as the place of birth.

The following search field allows a free text search by trial court.
Before using this field, please turn on search tips for this page and refer to our prison registers guide. This field should normally be left blank when searching for a named prisoner.

Prison register entries usually identify the court at which the prisoner was tried, and the date of the trial. A search for 'Perth', for example, will return any entries with a Perth trial court, e.g. Perth Sheriff Court or Perth Justice of the Peace.

Please note that records of trials heard by Scottish courts, if they survive, are not available to search on ScotlandsPeople. Please consult our prison registers guide for details on researching trial records.
