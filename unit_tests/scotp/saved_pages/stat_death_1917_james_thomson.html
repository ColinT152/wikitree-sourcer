Search Options

Women are indexed under their maiden surname and any married surname(s).

Enter the maiden surname or a previous married surname to narrow your search.

The index cannot distinguish between name types, for example, a search for a married woman named Brown with maiden surname Smith returns entries for a married woman Smith with maiden surname Brown.

If the maiden and married surnames are the same search on surname and leave this field blank.

Search Options

From 1974 onwards, this information was fully captured but only when the informant for the death was able to include such details on the death certificate. Between 1855 and 1973, the indexes for the mother’s maiden name, when recorded on the death certificate, and age at death are incomplete though we are working to populate these fields.

If you enter a name or age in this field and the information hasn't been included in the index the record will not be found. For deaths prior to 1974, we recommend that you carry out a search with the mother’s maiden name completed and if no results are returned then remove the mother's maiden name and search again. Remember you can reduce the number of search results by using location or the year of death range.

Search Options
Gender:

The default is both.

Select male or female to refine your search.

Enter a year range to narrow your search.

Information in other records can help you refine the year range, for example, if a parent is recorded as (deceased) on a statutory marriage record or an elderly person is no longer in a household in the following census.

Enter an age range to narrow your search.

The accuracy of information in a death entry is dependent on the informant who may not have known the exact age of the deceased.

Age at death is not included in some 1855-1865 index entries and is shown as 'U'. It is being added to the index retrospectively.

Select a county or city to narrow your search.

Select Minor Records to narrow your search to deaths overseas.

Displays all registration districts in Scotland unless a county, city or Minor Records has been selected.

If you selected a county or city the districts for that county or city are displayed. Select a district to narrow your search further.

If you selected Minor Records the registers and returns for deaths overseas are displayed. An overview of the Minor Records included in a death search is provided in the guide statutory register of deaths
