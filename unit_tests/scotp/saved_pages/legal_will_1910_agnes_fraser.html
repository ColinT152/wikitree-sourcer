Search Options

Enter start and finish years between 1513 and 1925 or leave these fields blank.

You can narrow your date range on the results page.

For testaments registered before 1600 the modern date is given (as if the year began on 1 January). For more information on this see our guide on dates, numbers and sums of money.

You can use the description field to search for a place name or someone's title or occupation. But please note that place names and occupations are not always given and spelling can vary.

For a list of occupations consult the glossary.

Select a court from the drop-down list.

Please note that some courts have several entries, for example, Edinburgh and Glasgow.

Some Sheriff Courts kept separate registers for inventories and wills, so the person you are looking for may appear twice in the index.
