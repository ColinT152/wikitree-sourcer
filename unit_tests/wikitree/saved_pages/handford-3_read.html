Biography

William was born in about September 1843 in High Bickington, Devon.[1]

When William was 21 he married Sophia Cole 5 December 1864 in High Bickington, Devon. He was a bachelor and she was a spinster but their son John was born before the marriage.[2][3]

The couple had 11 children: John William Handford aka Cole, Mary Elizabeth Handford, Marina Handford, Lucy Handford, Julia Handford, Thomas Handford, James Handford, Sophia Handford, Rosina Handford, Albert Handford and George William Handford.

An Ironstone Miner at Eston in 1891 living at 31 William St. Eston.[4]

William died at age 69 in 1913 in Middlesbrough.[5]
