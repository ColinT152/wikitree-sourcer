    Biography

    William Duncan Peffers was born about June 1898,in Bloemfontein, Orange Free State, South Africa, son of William Wilson Peffers and Annie (Brown) Peffers. [1]
    William Peffers served for the South African Republic in World War I
    Service started: unknown
    Unit(s): 4th Infantry Regiment
    Service ended: 19 Oct 1916

    He died on 19 October 1916, age 18 years at the Battle of Warlen Court, Somme, Picardy, France. [1] He is commemorated on the Thiepval Memorial, Somme, France.[2]
