Biography

Amelia was born on 4 June 1879 in Kentish Town, London, England. Date from 1939 Register.[1]

In the 1881 census Amelia was living in St Pancras, London, England. Relationship to Head: Daughter.[2]

On 3 May 1886 Amelia was living in London, England.[3]

In the 1891 census Amelia was living in St Pancras, London, England. Relation to Head of House: Daughter.[4]

Amelia married Arthur Wilson in January 1900 in Pancras, London. The couple had 2 children: Millicent Winifred Doris Wilson and Arthur Leslie Wilson.[5] [6]

In the 1901 census Amelia was living in Islington, London, England. Relation to Head: Wife.[7]

In the 1911 census Amelia was living in Tottenham, Middlesex, England. Marital Status: Married. Relation to Head of House: Wife.[8]

In the 1939 register Amelia was living in Southgate MB, Middlesex, England. 10 , Southgate M.B., Middlesex, England.[9]

Amelia died on 10 August 1958 in Edmonton, Middlesex, England.[10] [11]

There are additional sources with no facts or date. Often these are sources for the baptism or marriage of children that mention the parents or father. This section can just be removed if you will be adding those children and using these sources there.[12]
