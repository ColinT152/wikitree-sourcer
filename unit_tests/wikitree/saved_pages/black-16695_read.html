Biography

Louisa was born on 5 November 1886 in Aberdeen, Aberdeenshire, Scotland. Born at 7:30pm at 180 Hardgate, Aberdeen.[1][2][3]

In 1891 Louisa (age about 4) was living in Aberdeen St Nicholas, Aberdeenshire, Scotland. Relation to Head of House: Grand Daughter.[4]

In 1901 Louisa (age about 14) was living in Edinburgh Morningside, Midlothian, Scotland. Relation to Head of House: Daughter.[5]

In 1911 Louisa (age about 24) was living in Edinburgh, Midlothian, Scotland. Hospital Nurse in Training Royal Infirmary Edinburgh 1911 Census.

When Louisa was 35 she married Harold Edington Dickens on 21 September 1922 in Murree, Bengal, India. The couple had 2 children: Elizabeth L Dickens and Diana Grace Dickens.[6]

Louisa departed London, England on 31 January 1923.[7]

In 1927 Louisa (age about 40) was living in St Kilda Central, Balaclava, Victoria, Australia.[8]

Louisa departed Australia in January 1928 and arrived in London, England on 5 February 1928.[9]

In 1937 Louisa (age about 50) was living in Loughborough, Leicestershire. 1 Outwoods Road , Loughborough M.B., Leicestershire, England. With two children, H.E. not there.

Louisa died[10][11]at the age of 66 on 24 July 1953 in Bangor, Caernarvonshire, Wales. Caernarvonshire & Anglesey General Hospital.[3]
