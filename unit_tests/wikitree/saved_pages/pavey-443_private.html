Biography

Isabella was born in the July quarter of 1840 in E Stonehouse, Devon, England.[1]

In the 1841 census Isabella was living in East Stonehouse, Devon, England.[2]

In the 1851 census Isabella was living in Plymouth St Andrew, Devon, England. Relation to Head of House: Daughter.[3]

In the 1861 census Isabella was living in St Pancras, Middlesex, England. Relationship: Boarder.[4]

Isabella married Frederick Richardson on 31 January 1864 in Parish Chapel, St Pancras, England. The couple had one child: Emily Richardson.[5] [6]

In the 1871 census Isabella was living in St Pancras, London, England. Relationship: Daughter, Widow. Living with her father and her 5 year old daughter Emily.[7]

In 1872 Isabella was living in Battersea, Surrey, England. Marital Status: Widowed.[8] [9]

Isabella married John Smith Magenis on 9 September 1872 in St. Mary Battersea, Surrey, England.[10] [11] [12] [13] [14]

In 1891 Isabella was living in Auckland, Auckland, Auckland, New Zealand.[15]

In 1892 Isabella was living in Devonport, Waitakere, Auckland, New Zealand.[16]

In 1895 Isabella was living in Auckland, Auckland, New Zealand. 40 West Street, Auckland Central. Struck-off. Says widow, so may ne incorrect record. Diff record says 73 Grafton Road. Also widow.[17]

In 1896 Isabella was living in Auckland, Auckland, New Zealand.[18]

In 1897 Isabella was living in Auckland, Auckland, Auckland, New Zealand.[19]

In 1911 Isabella was living in Auckland, New Zealand. Dressmaker.

Isabella was buried in 1914 in Auckland, New Zealand.

Isabella died on 24 September 1914 in Auckland, Auckland, New Zealand. Obituary: "On September 24 at Costely Home, Isabella Magenis, late of Devonport.". https:/paperspast.natlib.govt.nz/newspapersAS19140925.2.28?query=Magenis.[20] [21]
