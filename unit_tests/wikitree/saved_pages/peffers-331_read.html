Biography

Christian Johnston Peffers was born on 20 March 1869 at School Wynd, Kirriemuir, daughter of Andrew Peffers and Jessie (Blamire) Peffers. [1]

She married John Milne on 13 July 1892 in Dundee. Witnesses were Peter Couper and Maggie Peffers.[2] They had 8 children.

  1. John Milne PEFFERS b.1891
  2. Jessie Blamire MILNE b.1894
  3. Elizabeth Ross MILNE b.1896
  4. Frances MILNE b.1898
  5. Margaret MILNE b.1900
  6. Emily MILNE b.1901
  7. Archibald Peffers MILNE b.1903
  8. Agnes MILNE b.1907

She appears in the following census years -

  • 1871 at Marywellbrae, Kirriemuir, Forfarshire. [3]
  • 1881 at 1 William Street, Forfar, Forfarshire. [4]
  • 1891 at Blackscroft, Dundee, Forfarshire where she was a jute spinner. [5]
  • 1901 at 34 Rostron Street, Manchester, Lancashire, England. [6]
  • 1911 at 15 Marsland Street, Ardwick, Lancashire, England. [7]
  • 1939 at 30 Hexham Road, Manchester, Lancashire, England. [8]

She died on 2 October 1943 in Manchester, Lancashire, age 74 years [9] and is buried in Gorton Cemetery, Manchester. [10]
