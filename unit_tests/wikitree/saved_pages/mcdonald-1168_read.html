Biography

Elizabeth Brown McDonald was born Nov 20 1869 in Georgia.

LIzzie B McDonald married Henry Pryor Almand, Jr Nov 15 1892 in Rockdale County, Georgia. [1][2]

1900 they were living in Militia District 631, Decatur (excl. Decatur town), DeKalb, Georgia.[3]

H P Almand Head Male 31 Georgia
Lizzie Almand Wife Female 31 Georgia
Pryor Almand Son Male 6 Georgia
Mattie Almand Daughter Female 2 Georgia

1910 they were living in Conyers, Rockdale, Georgia.[4]

Henry P Almand Head Male 41 Georgia
Lizzie B Almand Wife Female 40 Georgia
Otis P Almand Son Male 15 Georgia
Mattie L Almand Daughter Female 11 Georgia
Arthur E Almand Son Male 1 Georgia
Zillah A Almand Mother Female 74 Georgia

1920 they were living inConyers, Rockdale, Georgia.[5]

Henry P Almand Head Male 51 Georgia
Lizzie B Almand Wife Female 50 Georgia
Otis P Almand Son Male 25 Georgia
Mattie L Almand Daughter Female 21 Georgia
Arthur E Almand Son Male 11 Georgia
Lillian E Almand Daughter Female 7 Georgia
Zilla A Almand Mother Female 83 Georgia

She died Dec 24 1961 in Fulton County, Georgia. She is buried in Eastview Cemetery, Conyers, Rockdale County, Georgia[6]
