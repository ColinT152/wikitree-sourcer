Biography

Jane, daughter of John & Elizabeth, was baptised on 12 October 1840 in Boston, St Botolph, Lincolnshire, England.[1]

In the 1841 census Jane (age 8 Mo) was in Boston, Lincolnshire, England.[2]

In the 1851 census Jane (age 10) was the daughter of Elizabeth Brewster in Boston, Lincolnshire, England.[3]

Jane's marriage to Frederick Martin was registered in the Oct-Nov-Dec quarter of 1859 in the St Luke district.[4]

In the 1861 census Jane (age 20) was the wife of Fredk Martin in St Luke, Middlesex, England.[5]

Jane, daughter of William Ricards (her step-father), married Robert Young on 27 December 1876 in St James the Great, Bethnal Green, Tower Hamlets, England.[7]
