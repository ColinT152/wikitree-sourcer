Biography

Charles was born on 15 December 1825 in Atherstone, Warwickshire, England. He was the son of John Kimberlin and Elizabeth (Smith) Kimberlin.[1]

Charles was baptised on 6 January 1826 in Atherstone, Warwickshire, England.

When Charles was 25 he married on 26 April 1851 in Aston, Warwickshire, England. St. Peter and St. Paul Church in the parish of Aston.[2][3][4]
