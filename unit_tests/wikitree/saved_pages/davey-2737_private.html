Biography

Leonard was born in January 1841 in Taunton, Somerset, England.[1]

In the 1841 census Leonard was living in St Mary Magdalen, Somerset, England.[2]

In the 1851 census Leonard was living in Islington, Middlesex, England. Relationship: Son.[3]

In the 1861 census Leonard was living in Rotherhithe, Surrey, England. Relationship: Son.[4]

Leonard was married in October 1865 in Shoreditch, London.[5] [6]

In the 1871 census Leonard was living in St Phillip, London, England. Relation to Head: Nephew.[7]

In the 1881 census Leonard was living in St Mary Islington, London, England. Marital Status: Married. Relationship to Head: Head.[8]

In the 1891 census Leonard was living in Islington, London, England. Relation to Head: Head.[9]

In the 1901 census Leonard was living in Shoreditch, London, England. Relation to Head of House: Son.[10]

On 28 January 1904 Leonard was living in St Leonard, Shoreditch, London, England.[11] [12] [13]

Leonard died on 21 May 1907 in Claybury, Essex, England.[14] [15]

Leonard was buried on 25 May 1907 in Waltham Forest, Greater London, England.[16]
