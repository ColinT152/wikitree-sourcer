Biography

Betsy was born on 17 December 1830 in North Curry, Somerset, England. She was the daughter of Charles Pavey and Amelia (Holmes) Pavey.

Betsy was baptised on 29 December 1830 in North Curry, Somerset, England.[1] [2]

In the 1850 census Betsy (age 19) was living in Coldwater, Branch, Michigan, USA.[3]

When Betsy was 20 she married George Bentley Gardiner on 16 March 1851 in Branch, Michigan, USA. The couple had 3 children: Mary Ellen Gardiner, Joseph Bentley Gardiner and John Pavey Gardiner.[4] [5]

In the 1860 census Betsy (age 29) was living in El Dorado, El Dorado, California, USA. Residence Post Office: El Dorado.[6]

In the 1870 census Betsy (age 39) was living in Mud Springs, El Dorado, California, USA. Post Office: El Dorado.[7]

When Betsy was about 41 she married David E Norton in 1872 in Del Norte, California.[8]

In the 1880 census Betsy (age 49) was living in El Dorado, El Dorado, California, USA. Marital Status: Married. Relation to Head: Wife.[9]

Betsy died at the age of 54 on 4 February 1885 in Bakersfield, Kern, California, USA. From a private journal "Mrs. Betsy Ann Norton, sister of Mrs. H.P. Olds and Mrs. E. Willow and Mother of Mrs. D.R. Miller, died at the residence of Mr. Olds last Wednesday evening, after an illness of only a few days. She was here but a short time, on a visit, when she was taken with Pneumonia and medical skill proved of no avail. Her remains were taken to El Dorado, El Dorado County, for interment, and were accompanied by her two sisters, Mrs. Willow and Mrs. Olds, her brother Mr. Pavey; and her daughter Mrs. D.R. Miller, She leaves a large circle of friends who mourn her loss."[10]

Betsy was buried after 4 February 1885. El Dorado, El Dorado County, California, United States of America.[11]

Probate: 10 February 1885 in El Dorado, California, USA.[12]
